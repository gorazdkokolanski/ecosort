How to play
EcoSort

Do you think you know how to recycle?
The goal is to organize all the objects that belong to a recycling chain in their respective containers, until they are all in the container they belong to.

Drag the object from one container to another to complete the level.
The higher you get, the harder it gets, as time starts to run out and you have to organize all the containers more quickly

The E-Waste and Packaging fluxes contain different types of residues, so each type of residue has been separated by category into the 4 levels.
